5-Amino-1MQ (5mg)

5-Amino-1-methylquinolinium targets a metabolic bottleneck: nicotinamide N-methyltransferase (NNMT), an enzyme that consumes NAD+ precursors by methylating nicotinamide for excretion. By inhibiting NNMT, 5-Amino-1MQ redirects nicotinamide toward NAD+ biosynthesis, simultaneously increasing cellular NAD+ levels and reducing the accumulation of methylated metabolites. This dual mechanism makes it a key research tool for studying NNMT biology and NAD+ metabolism.
