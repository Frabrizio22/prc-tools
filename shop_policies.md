## 2. Return & Refund Policy

Due to the specialized nature of research compounds and contamination prevention protocols, PRC Peptides maintains a strict return policy to ensure product integrity.

### Return Eligibility

**Unopened, Sealed Products**
- Returns accepted within **14 calendar days** of delivery
- Product must be in original, unopened, manufacturer-sealed condition
- Tamper-evident seals must be fully intact
- Original packaging required

**Opened Products**
- **No returns or refunds** once seals are broken
- Contamination risk prevents resale or restocking
- This policy protects the integrity of our research-grade compounds

### Non-Returnable Circumstances
Returns will not be accepted for:
- Products opened or with broken seals
- Products stored improperly after delivery
- Orders placed in error after 14 days
- Custom or special orders (marked as such at checkout)

### Damaged or Incorrect Items
If you receive damaged, defective, or incorrect items:
- **Full replacement or refund** provided at no cost to you
- Must be reported within **7 days of delivery**
- Photographic evidence of damage may be requested
- We will provide a prepaid return label

### How to Initiate a Return

1. Email **support@prcpeptides.com** with:
   - Your order number
   - Item(s) you wish to return
   - Reason for return
   - Photos (if damaged/defective)

2. Await return authorization:
   - Our team will respond within 1-2 business days
   - You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
   - Do not ship items without an RMA number

3. Ship the return:
   - Package items securely in original packaging
   - Include RMA number clearly visible on package
   - Ship to the address provided in your RMA email

### Return Shipping Costs
- **Customer responsibility** for return shipping on buyer's remorse or ordering errors
- **PRC Labs covers costs** if the error is ours (wrong/damaged item)
- **No restocking fees** applied to any approved returns

### Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within **5-7 business days** of receiving and inspecting the returned item
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable (unless our error)
- You will receive email confirmation when your refund is processed
- Bank processing may take an additional 3-5 business days

### Refused Deliveries
Packages refused at delivery will be returned to our facility and processed as a return:
- Subject to return shipping fees
- Refund issued minus all shipping costs (outbound and return)
