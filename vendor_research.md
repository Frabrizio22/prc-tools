## PART 6: MARKET OBSERVATIONS

### Quality Indicators Found:
- Third-party lab testing (most active vendors)
- Certificate of Analysis (COA) availability
- GMP/cGMP facility claims
- 99%+ purity claims (standard)
- US-based manufacturing emphasis

### Common Features:
- Free shipping thresholds ($150-400)
- Same-day shipping options
- Cryptocurrency payment acceptance (10% discount common)
- Research-only disclaimers (FDA compliance)
- Multiple dosage sizes

### Price Ranges Observed:
- **BPC-157 (5mg):** $40-70
- **GLP-1/Semaglutide:** $50-375 (varies by dose)
- **Retatrutide:** $48-72 (base prices)
- **Tirzepatide:** $60-110
- **NAD+:** $30-65
- **TB-500:** $48-189

### Red Flags to Watch:
- Very minimal websites (like Instant Peptides)
- No visible pricing
- No COAs or lab testing mentioned
- International operations claiming "US vendor"
