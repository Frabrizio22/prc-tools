# PRC Peptides Slogan Strategy Research

## 1. COPYWRITING PRINCIPLES THAT WORK

### What Makes Slogans Effective:
1. **Brevity** - 3-7 words is the sweet spot (Nike: "Just Do It", Apple: "Think Different")
2. **Specificity** - Vague promises don't stick; specific claims do
3. **Differentiation** - Must separate you from competitors clearly
4. **Emotional Resonance** - Triggers a feeling (confidence, belonging, being smart)
5. **Memorability** - Rhythm, alliteration, unexpected phrasing
6. **Benefit-Forward** - What customer GETS, not what you DO
7. **Ownability** - Can't be stolen by competitors

### Psychological Triggers That Convert:
- **Social Proof** - "Chosen by..." / "Trusted by..."
- **Value Recognition** - "More for less" / "Stop overpaying"
- **Tribal Identity** - "For serious researchers" / "By researchers"
- **Simplicity** - "Everything included" / "No hassle"
- **Contrarian** - "While others... we..."
- **Transparency** - "Honest" / "No BS"

## 2. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE ANALYSIS

### What Competitors Say:
- **Swiss Chems**: "Same-Day Shipping, 99% Purity Guaranteed, Safe & Secure"
  - Generic safety/purity claims
  - Speed focus (same-day)
  
- **Limitless Life**: "Industry's Leading Source for 3rd Party Tested Research Peptides"
  - Authority claim ("leading source")
  - Quality focus (3rd party tested)
  
- **Strate Labs**: "Customer Focus, Integrity and Transparency, Innovation"
  - Corporate values word salad
  - Nothing ownable
  
- **Peptide Sciences**: "The Leader in Peptide Research"
  - Dominance claim (boring)
  - Generic authority

### What's OVERUSED (avoid):
- "Premium quality" / "highest quality"
- "Trusted source" / "industry leader"
- "99% purity" (everyone says this)
- "Integrity" / "transparency" (words without proof)
- "Innovation" (meaningless without context)
- "For your research" (obvious)

### THE WHITE SPACE:
Nobody talks about:
- **Complete solutions** (powder + everything you need)
- **Aggressive value** (half the price)
- **Honest comparison** (we review everyone, including ourselves)
- **Community-driven** (word of mouth growth)
- **Researcher-first mindset** (understanding the workflow)

## 6. RUNNER-UP COMBINATIONS

If "Everything included. Every order." feels too broad, consider:

### Option A (More Specific):
**"Peptides + recon kit. Every order."**
- Makes the kit explicit
- Slightly more tactical
- Less ownable long-term (what if competitors copy?)

### Option B (Value Emphasis):
**"Pure compounds. Honest prices. Kit included."**
- Covers multiple differentiators
- Longer (9 words, pushes limit)
- Less punchy

### Option C (Speed + Completeness):
**"Next day, everything included"**
- Combines two benefits
- Missing parallel structure punch
- Slightly awkward rhythm
