## ‚ùì COMMON APPLICATION QUESTIONS

**"What if I get declined everywhere?"**
- Try international/offshore processors (Instabill, DirectPayNet)
- Consider crypto-only operation
- Partner with established merchant (affiliate arrangement)
- Use cash/check/money order (old school)

**"Can I use multiple processors?"**
- Yes, but must disclose to each one
- Can split traffic (crypto + traditional)
- Can't use one as backup without disclosure

**"What about ACH/eCheck?"**
- Usually available with high-risk account
- Lower fees than cards (1-3%)
- Higher decline rate from customers
- 3-5 day settlement

**"Should I form an LLC?"**
- YES - protects personal assets
- Improves approval odds
- Professional appearance
- Tax benefits
